Mental health

Mental health is a kind of disease that can affect our thinking, feeling, and acting. Moreover, mental health does not only affect the things listed above, but also affect our daily life such as relationship, personality, and learning skills.

Anger and Depression

Anger is the issue that always make you feel angry or mad for no reasons. People are only angry when they are feeling bad about somethings such as decision, relationship and misunderstanding. But for people who are expressing anger, they can get angry for no reasons even everyone is nice to that person. Depression is that people always have low mood in daily and do some bad things such as self-harm, and self-esteem. In this modern society, depression can appear easily if people do not know the way to get out of stress from life.

Loneliness and suicidal feelings

Moreover, loneliness is the common mental health problem of people around the world, they just might need understanding from family and friends so give them plenty of time no matter how busy you are. Loneliness can lead patient to suicide themselves especially teenager who lost parents and no one understand them. Therefore, suicidal feeling is that people always think about killing themselves for no reasons. However, suicidal feelings only happens when people are really stressful about something and they cannot get out of the stress so they will think and imagine the way after killing themselves.

Causes of mentalth health issues

There are few elements that cause mental health problem to people. The most common element is childhood abuse and abuser can be parents or friends in school. In some countries, abusing children can be fined or go to jail such as America, Australia, and United Kingdom. Moreover, social isolation can also give mental health issue to people especially introvert people. Introvert people are people who is quite shy when they are social with a big group of people, some people will give them their own space but some will make fun of them so please give them some spaces and time. Therefore, racism and bereavement can be a part of mental health causes so please be respect, peace and, love to everyone no matter the color skin, nationality, and personality.

Kinds of mental health treatment

Firstly, psychotherapy can be the most common way to treat mental health problem. When people take psychotherapy, patient will talk with a mental health professional to find out reasons of getting mental health issues. Secondly, prescription medicine is the popular treatement for mental health problem. Mental health professioal will use medicine to make changes to brain chemical for a better result. Lastly, hospital treatment can be another way to suffer from mental health issue when the mental health become worse by day. People will need to stay long term in the hospital to get this kinds of treatment. Additionally, people can also have another way to treat themselves out of mental health such as doing exercise, going out with friends, and learning art
